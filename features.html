App Features

Real-time Location Tracking

Our real-time location tracking allows users to share their live location with trusted contacts. This feature ensures that, during emergencies or unsafe situations, users are always connected to help. With precise GPS coordinates and real-time updates, users can be located quickly, ensuring faster assistance when needed.

Emergency Contact Form

The emergency contact form allows users to store and manage important contacts who can be notified in case of emergencies. Users can add multiple contacts, who will receive alerts or notifications when the panic button is activated, ensuring that the right people are informed immediately.

Panic Button

The panic button is a core safety feature of the app. By pressing this button, users can instantly send distress signals to their emergency contacts, alerting them of any danger. The alert includes the user's real-time location, allowing emergency responders or trusted contacts to provide timely assistance.

Safety Alerts

Safety alerts notify users of any ongoing threats or dangerous situations in their vicinity. This feature uses real-time data to update users about crime reports, accidents, or any public safety concerns, helping users stay informed and avoid risky areas.
